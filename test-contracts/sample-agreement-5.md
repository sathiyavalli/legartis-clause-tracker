# SUPPLY AGREEMENT

## INTRODUCTION

This Supply Agreement is entered into between Superior Manufacturing Corp ("Supplier") and Global Distribution Inc ("Buyer") as of March 13, 2026.

## PRODUCT SPECIFICATIONS

Supplier shall provide high-quality industrial components according to the specifications outlined in the attached technical documentation. All products shall be manufactured to industry standards and subject to Buyer's inspection and acceptance.

## GOVERNING LAW AND JURISDICTION

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the Federal Republic of Germany. The parties irrevocably agree to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts located in Berlin for any disputes arising from this Agreement.

## WARRANTY PROVISIONS

Supplier warrants that all Products shall be free from defects in materials and workmanship for a period of twelve (12) months from the date of delivery. Supplier further warrants that the Products do not infringe any patents, trademarks, or copyrights of third parties and that Supplier has full authority to sell the Products.

## LIMITATION OF LIABILITY AND DAMAGES CLAUSE

UNDER NO CIRCUMSTANCES SHALL EITHER PARTY BE LIABLE FOR ANY INDIRECT, INCIDENTAL, SPECIAL, CONSEQUENTIAL, OR PUNITIVE DAMAGES. THE TOTAL LIABILITY OF SUPPLIER SHALL NOT EXCEED THE AMOUNTS PAID BY BUYER IN THE PREVIOUS 12 MONTHS.

## TERMINATION FOR CONVENIENCE

Either party may terminate this Agreement without cause upon sixty (60) days prior written notice. The terminating party shall provide written notice specifying the effective termination date and reason for termination if applicable.

## CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

Each party acknowledges that it may receive confidential information from the other party, including trade secrets, technical data, pricing information, and business strategies. The receiving party agrees to protect such information with reasonable security measures and shall not disclose it to any third party without consent.

## FORCE MAJEURE PROTECTION

Neither party shall be held liable for any failure or delay in performance under this Agreement caused by circumstances beyond reasonable control, including natural disasters, war, terrorism, or epidemic disease.

## INSURANCE COVERAGE

Supplier shall maintain property insurance covering the Products while in Supplier's possession. Supplier shall also maintain liability insurance with minimum coverage of Two Million Dollars ($2,000,000). Proof of insurance shall be provided upon request.

## NON-COMPETE CLAUSE

For the duration of this Agreement and for three (3) years after termination, Supplier shall not directly or indirectly engage in business activities that compete with Buyer's operations in Western Europe without Buyer's written consent.

## INDEMNIFICATION

Supplier shall indemnify and defend Buyer against all third-party claims arising from defective Products, infringement of intellectual property rights, or breach of this Agreement.

## GOOD FAITH AND COOPERATION

Both parties agree to perform their obligations in good faith and to cooperate reasonably to achieve the purposes of this Agreement. Neither party shall take actions intended to prevent the other party from performing its obligations.
