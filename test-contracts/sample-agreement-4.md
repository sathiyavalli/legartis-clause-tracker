# CONSULTING SERVICES AGREEMENT

Date: March 2026

## STATEMENT OF SERVICES

This Consulting Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between Consulting Associates LLC ("Consultant") and DataCorp Industries ("Client").

### ENGAGEMENT SCOPE

The Consultant shall provide consulting services related to digital transformation, business process optimization, and technology strategy. Services shall be provided on an as-needed basis as requested by the Client, with detailed statements of work prepared prior to each engagement.

## PAYMENT AND INVOICING

The Client shall pay Consultant at the rate of Two Hundred Fifty Dollars ($250) per hour for all consulting services rendered. Invoices shall be submitted monthly for services performed, and payment shall be due within fifteen (15) business days of invoice receipt. Any overdue payments shall accrue daily interest at a rate of 1.5% per month.

## CONFIDENTIALITY

Consultant acknowledges receiving access to highly sensitive Client business information, strategic plans, financial data, and customer lists. Consultant agrees to maintain strict confidentiality of all information and shall not disclose any confidential information to third parties without prior written authorization from Client. Consultant further agrees that confidential information shall not be used for any purpose other than to perform services under this Agreement. This confidentiality obligation shall continue for seven (7) years following termination of the Agreement.

## LIMITATION OF LIABILITY

Under no circumstances shall Consultant's total liability to Client under this Agreement exceed the total fees paid by Client in the immediately preceding twelve (12) month period. In no event shall either party be liable to the other for any lost profits, lost revenue, lost data, business interruption, or any other indirect, incidental, special, or consequential damages, regardless of whether such party was advised of the possibility of such damages or how those damages arose.

## FORCE MAJEURE

If either party is prevented from performing its obligations under this Agreement due to circumstances beyond a party's reasonable control, including but not limited to natural disasters (earthquake, flood, hurricane), acts of war, terrorism, pandemic or epidemic, government sanctions, or infrastructure failures, then that party's obligations shall be suspended for as long as the force majeure event prevents performance, provided that the affected party promptly notifies the other party of the event and uses reasonable efforts to resume performance.

## INSURANCE AND RISK MANAGEMENT

Consultant shall maintain comprehensive professional liability insurance covering the services provided hereunder with minimum limits of One Million Dollars ($1,000,000) per occurrence. Consultant shall provide proof of insurance within ten (10) business days of request and shall notify Client within forty-eight (48) hours of any changes to insurance coverage or claims filed. All risks of loss or damage to Client property shall be borne by Consultant while in Consultant's possession or control.

## WARRANTIES AND REPRESENTATIONS

Consultant represents and warrants that: (1) Consultant has the full authority and right to enter into this Agreement; (2) Consultant has the skills, experience, and expertise to perform the services as described; (3) all work product will be original unless otherwise specified; (4) all services shall comply with all applicable laws and regulations; (5) the services shall not infringe any intellectual property rights of third parties.

## TERMINATION PROVISIONS

Client may terminate this Agreement for any reason or no reason upon thirty (30) days' written notice to Consultant. Consultant may terminate this Agreement due to Client's material breach if Client fails to cure the breach within fifteen (15) days of written notice. Upon termination, Client shall pay Consultant for all services rendered through the termination date, including reimbursable expenses.

## GOVERNING LAW

This Agreement shall be governed by and interpreted in accordance with the laws of the State of New York, without regard to conflicts of law principles. All disputes shall be resolved through binding arbitration in New York unless parties mutually agree otherwise.

## NON-COMPETE

During the term of this Agreement and for eighteen (18) months thereafter, Consultant shall not provide services to any of Client's direct competitors in the technology consulting or business process optimization sectors within the geographic area of North America and Europe.

## GENERAL PROVISIONS

All notices required under this Agreement must be in writing and shall be effective when delivered personally, sent by email with confirmation of receipt, or sent by certified mail to the addresses specified below. This Agreement may only be amended in writing signed by both parties.
