# Sample Service Agreement

## 1. PARTIES TO THE AGREEMENT

This Agreement is entered into on [Date] by and between:

**Provider:** ABC Consulting Services, Inc.
**Client:** XYZ Corporation

## 2. SERVICES TO BE PROVIDED

The Provider agrees to supply consulting services including but not limited to:

- Strategic planning and analysis
- Process optimization
- Technology implementation support
- Training and knowledge transfer

## 3. COMPENSATION AND PAYMENT TERMS

### 3.1 Fees
The Client agrees to pay the Provider the following fees:

- Consulting Rate: $250 per hour
- Project Management: $150 per hour
- Travel expenses: Reimbursed at cost

### 3.2 Payment Schedule
- 50% upfront payment upon contract execution
- 25% upon completion of Phase 1
- 25% upon final delivery and acceptance

## 4. CONFIDENTIALITY AND NON-DISCLOSURE

Both parties agree to maintain strict confidentiality regarding:

- Proprietary information and trade secrets
- Client data and business processes
- Project methodologies and approaches
- Financial terms and pricing information

## 5. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY RIGHTS

### 5.1 Work Product
All work product, deliverables, and materials created specifically for this project shall become the property of the Client upon full payment.

### 5.2 Pre-existing Materials
Each party retains ownership of their pre-existing intellectual property, tools, and methodologies.

## 6. TERM AND TERMINATION

### 6.1 Term
This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue for a period of 12 months unless terminated earlier.

### 6.2 Termination for Convenience
Either party may terminate this Agreement with 30 days written notice.

### 6.3 Termination for Cause
Either party may terminate immediately upon material breach by the other party.

## 7. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY

Notwithstanding anything to the contrary, in no event shall either party be liable for:

- Indirect, incidental, or consequential damages
- Lost profits or business interruption
- Data loss or corruption
- Third-party claims

Maximum liability shall not exceed the total fees paid under this Agreement.

## 8. GOVERNING LAW AND DISPUTE RESOLUTION

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of California. Any disputes shall be resolved through binding arbitration in accordance with the rules of the American Arbitration Association.

## 9. INSURANCE REQUIREMENTS

The Provider shall maintain the following insurance coverage:

- Professional Liability Insurance: $1,000,000 per occurrence
- General Liability Insurance: $1,000,000 per occurrence
- Workers' Compensation Insurance as required by law

## 10. FORCE MAJEURE

Neither party shall be liable for failure to perform due to causes beyond reasonable control including:

- Natural disasters
- War or civil unrest
- Government regulations
- Internet or telecommunications failures

## 11. INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR STATUS

The Provider is an independent contractor and not an employee, agent, or partner of the Client. The Provider shall be responsible for all taxes, insurance, and other obligations.

## 12. NON-COMPETE AND NON-SOLICITATION

During the term of this Agreement and for 12 months thereafter, the Provider agrees not to:

- Compete directly with the Client's business
- Solicit the Client's employees or customers
- Disclose confidential information to competitors

## 13. WARRANTIES AND REPRESENTATIONS

### 13.1 Provider Warranties
The Provider warrants that:
- Services will be performed professionally and competently
- Deliverables will meet the specifications outlined
- Work will not infringe on third-party intellectual property

### 13.2 Client Warranties
The Client warrants that:
- They have authority to enter into this Agreement
- Information provided is accurate and complete
- They will cooperate in the performance of services

## 14. INDEMNIFICATION

Each party agrees to indemnify and hold harmless the other party from claims arising from:

- Breach of this Agreement
- Negligence or willful misconduct
- Intellectual property infringement
- Violation of applicable laws

## 15. MISCELLANEOUS PROVISIONS

### 15.1 Entire Agreement
This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the parties and supersedes all prior agreements.

### 15.2 Amendments
Any amendments must be in writing and signed by both parties.

### 15.3 Severability
If any provision is held invalid, the remainder shall remain in effect.

### 15.4 Notices
All notices shall be in writing and delivered to the addresses specified above.

### 15.5 Counterparts
This Agreement may be executed in counterparts, each of which shall be deemed an original.
